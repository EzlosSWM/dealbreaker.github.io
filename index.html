How To Play?

Deal Breaker is a party game where each player is dealt 3 cards: 2 blue cards and 1 red card.
The white cards represent the good qualities of a person A.K.A. "perks" and the red cards are "deal breakers".
In order to play, each player will deal their respective hands. Everyone would have to pick which of the people dealt they would prefer to date.
